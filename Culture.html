Gojani's Illustrations
'Coloring Prishtina' is a cooperation between the Municipality of Prishtina and 2cm Humans. This fusion came together to combine the love for the city with a 2cm small universe that was born out of love. The illustrated scenes reveal monuments, events, and important stories of the city of Prishtina, put together with short descriptions that carry that importance to each of us, and makes our love for our city grow.

Nora e Kelmendit - 2020
A mini encyclopedia of Pristina, serving as a tour guide as well. It follows the same format as an alphabet book, where for almost every letter of the Albanian alphabet there are illustrations and descriptions for monuments, people, activities, and business that contribute to the socio-cultural aspects of the capital.

"The first illustration of this style that I did was Baresha. Because it was so well received by the online audience, I continued to illustrate other characters from our folklore and literature ”.

2cm Humans is a duo working on bringing to life a universe illustrated in only 2cm Humans, and putting everything else to scale with that. 2cm Humans have worked on various projects, and also worked with the Municipality of Prishtina to bring out the first coloring book of the city of Prishtina called 'Coloring Prishtina'.
